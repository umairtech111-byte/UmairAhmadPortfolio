About Me

Mechanical BIM Engineer with International Project Experience

I am a detail-oriented Mechanical BIM Engineer based in Peshawar, Pakistan, with professional experience delivering MEP Revit models and BIM coordination for international US-based projects at BIMSERV | BIMTEQ (American Houston HQ).

My core expertise lies in HVAC Ducting & Piping Modeling and Detailing — designing and coordinating duct layouts, chilled water systems, ventilation networks, and plumbing infrastructure using Autodesk Revit MEP at LOD 400–450 standards.

Beyond BIM and MEP, I bring strong mechanical engineering fundamentals backed by advanced simulation skills in SolidWorks, ANSYS, Abaqus, and COMSOL Multiphysics — covering FEA structural analysis and CFD fluid dynamics.

I hold a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from UET Mardan with a CGPA of 3.53 (2021–2025). Actively seeking BIM Engineer / MEP Revit Modeler opportunities in the GCC region.
